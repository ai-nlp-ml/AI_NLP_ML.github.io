How to reach IIT Patna
Bihta is approx 25Km west of Patna. Following are the options to reach IIT Patna.

Train: Bihta station is on Patna-Delhi main railway line. There are many trains (local/express) that stops at Bihta. From station you can take auto to reach IIT Patna. Fare for share auto is Rs. 5-10/- and for reserve they take Rs. 50-80/- depending upon the time.

Bus: There is a direct BSRTC bus from Gandhi Maidan Patna to IIT Patna Gate. It runs at regular interval during 6am - 6pm. There is no bus service at night. Route of the bus is
Gandhi Maidan => Patna Jn railway station (Platform 1 side) => Danapur railway station => IIT Patna.

Taxi/Cab: You can also opt for taxi or cab (ola). They usually take around Rs. 1500/- for the trip.
