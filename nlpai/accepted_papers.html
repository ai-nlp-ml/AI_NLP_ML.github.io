Guidelines for Camera-ready Submission

1. Authors must follow the ACL Author Guidelines

2. Authors must download the ACL 2020 style sheets from ICON 2020 mainpage and uncomment line 26 (\aclfinalcopy) in the 'acl2020.tex' file.

3. The deadline for submission of both long and short papers is 7 December, 2020 (GMT -12).

4. The final submission site will be available at https://www.softconf.com/icon2020/papers/ from 1 December, 2020 to 7 December, 2020
