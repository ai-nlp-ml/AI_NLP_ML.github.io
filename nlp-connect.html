NLP Connect Talk Series


The NLP Connect Talk Series aims to connect the scholarly community to know about recent NLP/ML research, foster collaborations, and imbibe cross-fertilization of ideas.

We invite researchers in AI/NLP/ML to:

a) briefly talk about their research or something they feel excited to look forward in the community

b) motivate our students and researchers to think beyond and know about the latest state of progress in NLP/ML

The talks are usually informal in nature and of 45 minutes-1 hour in duration with a Q&A session (10-15 mins). With the consent of the speaker, we record these talks and host them on our youtube channel for the community. We would like to invite interested NLP/ML researchers to be a part of NLP Connect.

Please contact Tirthankar Ghosal (tirthankar[DOT]slg[AT]gmail[DOT]com) if you have any questions or suggestions.
