About Me

Researcher in Scholarly Language Processing (AI/NLP/ML/Digital Libraries)

I primarily work on mining scholarly scientific articles, extract meaningful information from them with AI/NLP/ML techniques. Our objective is to cut through the clutter of science in academia and accelerate scientific discovery. Using Natural Language Processing, Machine Learning-Deep Learning, Bibliometric Intelligence techniques we seek to develop an AI that would help humans understand scientific knowledge better and connect the dots faster.


My current research is dedicated towards three scholarly problems: Novelty, Scope and Quality of Scientific Articles. Specifically, we  seek to develop an AI that would assist editors/reviewers in the peer review process.


Research to me is an intellectual pilgrimage.
